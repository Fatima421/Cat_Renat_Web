About the app

Our app has been created to spread content in Catalan and at the same time promote and encourage the use of the Catalan language.

It is aimed at the general audience, there is no age restriction, anyone can inform themselves through the sections created. For this reason, there is also a variety of sections, we have a section dedicated to Catalan music, another to Catalan series, films, and documentaries, another about the available games translated to Catalan, and finally Catalan books.

Through this app, you can find out on which platform is all this content or where to buy it. It provides links to all sites, and it's made with an intuitive and user-friendly design.
