Find all type of content in Catalan

CATàleg is an app created with the intention of spreading content in Catalan and at the same time being able to promote and encourage the use of the language. Through this app you can find out on which platform is all this content or where to buy it.

  • Search and filter the results with the search bar
  • Save your favourite content marking the heart in the sections
  • Watch the trailers of the video games and audiovisuals
  • Modify your user as you wish by accessing the lateral menu
DETAILS

Every section in detail

You can select the item of your interest and consult the information about it in detail. There is a brief description apart from links and images.

In the audiovisual screen, you can access the corresponding platform, in the video game platform you can go to see the page that makes the official translation of the game in Catalan, in literature, you can go buy the book, and finally, in music, you have the opportunity to listen to the song on YouTube.

Categories

The different categories offered by CATàleg allow you to discover a world of content in Catalan in literature, multimedia, and video games.

  • Music

    Find and browse songs with your artists and albums and play them on YouTube.

  • Video games

    Select your platform, watch and discover different video games in detail..

  • Audiovisuals

    Find the content of your interest in movies, series, documentaries, and more.

  • Literature

    Browse through genre lists to find new stories that will hook you.
